## Backlog Management Notes

### Prioritization Criteria

Stories are prioritized based on:
1. **Business Value** — Impact on users or revenue
2. **Dependencies** — What blocks other work
3. **Risk** — Technical uncertainty or complexity
4. **Size** — Smaller stories prioritized to maintain flow

### Estimation Guidelines

- **1 point** = 1 hour of focused work (simple, well-understood)
- **2 points** = 2 hours (straightforward, minor complexity)
- **3 points** = 3-4 hours (moderate complexity)
- **5 points** = 5-6 hours (complex, multiple components)
- **8 points** = 8+ hours (very complex, consider splitting)
- **13 points** = Too big, must split into smaller stories
